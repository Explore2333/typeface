About North America

North America is the third-largest continent and home to a diverse range of cultures and landscapes. It covers about 16% of the Earth's total land area!

Best Places to Visit in North America

Grand Canyon National Park (Arizona, USA)

This canyon spans 277 river miles and is about a mile deep. It is a geological marvel where visitors can hike its rugged trails, take a mule ride on the canyon floor, or gaze over the edge at viewpoints.

Chichén Itzá (Yucatán, Mexico)

This is a World Heritage site and one of the Seven World Wonders. It is known for its masterful architecture in the ancient Mayan city. El Castillo, the temple there, shows the Mayans' advanced astronomical knowledge.

Banff National Park (Alberta, Canada)

This is Canada's first national park in the heart of the Canadian Rockies. It's known for its dramatic peaks, dense pine forests, and turquoise glacial lakes. It is great for hiking and wildlife viewing.

New York City (New York, USA)

This is the cultural and financial heartbeat of the US offers endless exploration. The Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, and more are definitely attractions to cover.
