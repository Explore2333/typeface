About Africa

Africa is the second largest and second most-populous continent after Asia. It covers about 20% of the Earth's total land area!

Best Places to Visit in South America

Victoria Falls (Zambia/Zimbabwe)

It is a massive natural wonder that is twice as deep and wide as Niagara. It is best to visit in April and May for peak water flow!

Cape Town, South Africa

This has world-class beaches with a bustling food scene, and iconic landmarks like Table Mountain Ascot Tours.

Serengeti National Park, Tanzania

This is a legendary park known for the Great Migration, where many animals traverse the savannah.

Okavango Delta, Botswana

This is known as the 'Jewel of the Kalahari', the delta swells with seasonal rains and a breathtaking maze of channels and islands.

Sahara Desert (Morocco/Algeria)

This is spanning across northern Africa and is known for its burnt-orange dunes.

Marrakesh, Morocco

Located in northwest Africa, this city dazzles visitors through its historical palaces and architecture.

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Uganda

This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a dense forest celebrated for its biodiversity and offers rare opportunities to go gorilla trekking to see endangered mountain gorrilas!
