About Australia

Australia is the smallest continent and the largest island in the world. It is located in the Southern Hemisphere and is surrounded by the Indian and Pacific Oceans!

Best Places to Visit in South America

Sydney Opera House & Harbour (NSW)

This is a UNESCO World Heritage site with gleaming sail-like structures of the Opera House defining the metropolis.

The Great Barrier Reef (QLD)

This is the world's largest coral reef system, even visible from space. It is a destination from snorkelers and divers wanting to explore!

Uluru(NT)

This is a sandstone monolith in the Red Centre. It is famous for its Indigenous cultural significance and breathtaking colors at sunrise and sunset.

Great Ocean Road (VIC)

This is famous for its winding coastal drives featuring the Twelve Apostles, stunning ocean views, and the ancident Otway Rainforest.

Kangaroo Island (SA)

This is a wildlife destination offering pristine wilderness where you can spot kangaroos, koalas, echidnas, and more in their natural habitat.
