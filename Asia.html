Best Places to Visit in South America

Angkor Wat (Cambodia)

This is the largest religious monument in the world, being a stone temple with intricate Hindu-Buddhist carvings.

The Great Wall of China (China)

This is an ancient stone fortification stretching thousands of miles across mountain ridges.

Taj Mahal (India)

This is a 17th century white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife. This is celebrated as an Indo-Islamic architecture.

Mount Fuji (Japan)

This is an iconic, symmetrical snow-capped stratovolcano that stands as Japan's highest peak. It is a cultural symbol and popular for those who want to go trekking.

Petronas Twin Towers (Malaysia)

They are 88-story twin skyscrapers in Kuala Lumper that dominate the skyline with a futuristic steel and glass fascade and public skybridge.

Marina Bay Sands (Singapore)

This is a luxury resort complex famous for its three hotal towers connected at the top by a massive, boat-shaped sky park and infinity pool.
