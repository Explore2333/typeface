About Antarctica

Antarctica is the southernmost continent and is almost entirely covered in ice. It is the coldest, driest, and windiest continent on Earth.

Best Places to Visit in South America

Antarctic Peninsula

This is the most accessible part of the mainland with jagged mountain peaks, wildlife, and glaciers.

Deception Island

This is an active volcanic caldera with a flooded center where visitors can explore historic abadoned whaling stations at Whalers Day and take a swim in the hot springs.

South Georgia Island

This is an island known for its massive king penguin colonies, fur seal populations, and a whaling station at Grytviken.

Lemaire Channel

This is a narrow but breathtaking strait lined by cliffs and icebergs. It is known as Kodak's Gap for its scenery.

The South Pole

This is the bottom of the Earth. It can be reached with specialized fly-in expeditions, allowing visitors to attend.
