About South America

South America is the fourth-largest continent and home to diverse cultures and landscapes. It covers about 12% of the Earth's total land area!

Best Places to Visit in South America

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is perched in the Andes Montains. It is a historial site of the Incan civilization and one of the world's most recognized historical sites.

Galapagoss Islands, Ecuador

This is an isolated volanic archipelago located in the Pacific Ocean. It is known for its wildlife inspiring Charle Darwin's theory of evolution.

Iguazu Falls, Argentina & Brazil

This is located on the border of Argentina and Brazil, and is one of the largest waterfall systems in the world.

Salar de Uyuni, Bolivia

This is located in the Andes Mountains, and is the world's largest salt flat. It becomes a massive reflective mirror in the rainy season.

Patagonia, Argentina & Chile

This is a populated region in the southern tip of South America that has jagged peaks, massive glaciers, and turquoise lakes. It is known for its outdoor activities.

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

This is a coastal metropolis known for its natural setting between lush mountains and the Atlantic ocean. It is known for its iconic landmarks.

Atacama Desert, Chile

This is one of the most driest and highest deserts in the world. It is known for its red rock formations, geysers, and valleys. It's great for stargazing.
